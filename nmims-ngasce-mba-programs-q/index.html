FAQ Related to NMIMS

1. Is the NMIMS Distance Program is valid for the WES ?

The programs are tailored to professionals who want to learn while working in a distance learning environment. The Programs are recognized by WES because NMIMS University is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). Students can verify the same information on the WES Portal for Canadian immigration.



2. In which cities NMIMS Distance Exam Centres exists?

Narsee Monjee Distance Learning Exams are held at Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Delhi, Gurugram, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Indore, Jaipur, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik, Navi Mumbai, New Delhi, Noida, Patna, Pune, Raipur, Rajkot, Surat, Thane, Vadodara subject to the final decision by University in the corresponding Exam Session.



3. Is it possible to receive a loan to pay for Narsee Monjee’s distance MBA fees?

NGASCE provides a comprehensive choice of courses in all areas of IT and Management Education to aspiring young minds. Fees for NMIMS Distance Learning MBA can be paid in convenient monthly installments using credit cards issued by top Indian banks. Students can also use the University’s loan facility to pay for their Narsee Monjee Distance Learning MBA fees.
