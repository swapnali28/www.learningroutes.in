]]> From hand-written mails to ultra-modern AR classrooms, distance education has certainly come a long way. For this long and lasting journey, we give the entire credit (without any second thoughts) to the trailblazing technology and the quenchless thirst for knowledge. But more importantly, the trailblazing technology. Through the sophisticated yet simpler mediums of e-lectures, televisions, podcasts, and virtual classrooms, educational institutions deliver ODL courses to students located in the remotest corners at a cost much lower than the regular courses. Distance education lets a student juggle between work and study, all at the same time. So, next time, when you are stuck under a truckload of work and still wish to pursue an MBA without resigning, you are definitely not out of your head. Not at all. We assure you that. 

Recently, the University Grants Commission (UGC) released a notification that has brought Open and Distance Learning (ODL) Learning at par with Regular Learning. Without an ounce of doubt, the notification has added to the credibility of the distance learning mode. Let us not forget that and also the fact that better job offers are awaiting us as soon as we update our resume with a Distance MBA.

Why MBA?

No one can deny the fact that an MBA degree on a resume is a spectacular sight. Also, no one, absolutely no one can deny that a stellar MBA degree pays you much more than just a dream job with a rewarding salary. It equips you with a powerful combination of academic, professional, and personal development lessons. 

Top Colleges for ODL MBA in India: 

The entire process of choosing the right college can be intimidating, especially when we are swamped with oceans of options and literally have no time in the world to do a detailed analysis of every single college so as to take an ‘informed’ decision. It can be pretty daunting. It is. 

For hopeless situations like these, we have come up with a short, crisp list of top-notch colleges offering stellar Distance MBA programmes in India. Here it goes:

1. Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies Global Access School for Continuing Education (NGA-SCE)

If you are looking for an invigorating learning experience while getting a specialised MBA degree, then you should swoop at once to the NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education. Set up in the year 1994, the NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education offers as many as ten specialisations under its ODL MBA segment. The institution disseminates education via the format of live as well as recorded lectures and discussion forums to build an ecosystem, where teachers and students can interact with one another and amongst themselves. 

The university will definitely not leave you with only top-rated education. With its unparalleled career services and vast industry networking, the university endeavours to assist its students in designing the right framework for their careers under the guidance of professional experts.

2. Symbiosis (SCOL)

The UGC-approved, MBA offered by the Symbiosis Centre is dynamic and contemporary. Belonging to the eminent Symbiosis Society, they offer an extensive curriculum that is in alignment with the looming demands of the industry. The curriculum is delivered through digitally-enabled resources such as live classrooms, chats, e-resources, etc. 

Besides providing an array of MBA specialisations to choose from, the centre allows its students to schedule their examinations when they feel ready, keeping in mind their work commitments and less time for preparation. Through its strong industry-academia linkage, the centre facilitates students to broaden their academic horizons as well as professional networking. 

3. Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU)

Only one name echoes in mind when one thinks of the pioneer behind distance education in India- the Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). With its gigantic beehive of regional and study centres, the university aims to circulate its educational services to every single nook and cranny of India. It offers AICTE-approved Distance MBA Programmes at a reasonable fee in ultra-inventive formats. 

The university has set up a learning management application for its students to provide them with convenient access to industry-relevant e-resources. The audio and video lectures are circulated via its exclusive radio and tv channels.  

4. Amity (ADDOE)

Industry-academia interaction is of the utmost importance when it comes to building prominent professional connections for stepping up onto the career ladder. And no university, other than the widely respected Amity University, can perform this task better. To help its students grow and evolve in this volatile industrial world, it organises Thinktalks, guest lectures, job fairs, and whatnot!

The directorate provides students with well-structured modules. The modules equip students with skills to find multi-disciplinary solutions to current global industrial issues. Recognised by the Distance Education Bureau (DEB) of the University Grants Commission (UGC), the distance MBA of the Amity is a lucrative career opportunity for professionals as well as others. 

5. Suresh Gyan Vihar University Directorate of Distance Education

You are enrolled in an impressive Distance MBA programme and have hands-on experience in a project involving your dream industry. Also, you are also earning an additional certification from a global educational institution. Also, you are not dreaming because all of this is absolutely possible at Suresh Gyan Vihar University.  The university’s Directorate of Distance Education (DDE) offers an assortment of dynamic MBA programmes in distance education mode. The programmes are offered via a proficient learning management portal where extensive virtual classes are available for the students in addition to e-books and assessments. 

One of the most promising features of the university that gives it an edge over other universities is that it offers its students a lucrative option to upgrade their MBA degrees with outstanding certifications from its academic and industrial partners. 

Takeaway

Doing an MBA degree is a massive investment of time, money, and effort. A student needs to go through hoards of information before selecting the right college. We hope that this blog has helped to minimise the herculean task and present the best possible guidance to the students.
