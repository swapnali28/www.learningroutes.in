]]> It is rightly said that today data is driving all the major businesses of the world. The world in total generates 40 trillion gigabytes of data daily, so, a single individual generates 1.7 megabytes of data per second. A recent study done by Splunk points out that the quantity of data will grow nearly 5 times by 2025. Technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and 5G are set to increase the access to data we currently possess. This forthcoming explosion of data opens innumerable opportunities for youth in future.

The Emerging Field of Business Analytics

Business Analytics refers to the study of analysis and interpretation of a company’s data to prepare an optimum business strategy for them. It focuses exclusively on analytics and is suitable for young minds who have an aptitude for both information technology and business management. Companies that are totally reliant on data treat it as an asset, thus, they look for skilled professionals who can deal with it efficiently. 

Scope of Business Analytics

For any company, it is utmost important to stay relevant and ahead of its competitors. So, they constantly innovate their business. They ditch old, time-consuming methods and innovate better ones to meet their business demands. As methods are innovated, the amount of data for companies pile up. This is where business analytics step in. If a company’s data is handled efficiently, that company is bound to flourish. Analytics help  to make informed business decisions.

According to a study by Analytics India Industry, the industry in  India is increasing at a considerable rate of 23.8% per year.  BBA in Business Analytics helps a student to acquire the skill-set like statistical analysis, data mining, forecasting, text analytics, data visualisation and much more to read data and strategise into a business plan for the companys’ success. 

NMIMS Global BBA in Business Analytics 

NMIMS offers a 3-year BBA in Business Analytics in Open and Distance Learning Mode. The program is specially designed to equip students with the concept of business management, data analytics and data visualisation across all functional domains of a company. The program offers a perfect blend of academics and industry-specific knowledge that ensures that the students get real-time experience. It gives hands-on experience to its students to teach them to deal with both structured and unstructured data. It is designed to equip students to become managers and business analysts at entry level positions. 

NMIMS Global BBA in Business Analytics ( Distance Mode) uses real-world case studies to teach business concepts and prepares its students to use analytical tools like Python, Excel and Tableau. The course is spread over 6 semesters with 6 subjects taught in every semester. The program is packed with modules in soft-skills, technical skills and business management skills.

Eligibility and Fees 

10+2 in any field from a recognised board with a minimum of 50% marks

Or

10+2 in any field from a recognised board with a minimum of 45% marks and at least 2 years of work experience

Or

SSC (10) + 3 years Diploma recognised by AICTE with a minimum of 55% marks

There are three flexible options to pay the required course fee.

NMIMS offers 20% concession to candidates who belong to the armed forces. 

Students can also avail the educational loan facility and the EMI facility from the banks. 

Features and Outcomes 

Employability Skills

NMIMS has added a unique module to its program structure which lays special emphasis on developing students’ employability skills by honing their soft and technical skills. It has added the module ‘Soft Skills for Managers’ in the first semester. Similarly, it has added modules like ‘Design Thinking’ and ‘Start Your Start Up’ in the second and third semester respectively which helps students execute the knowledge they have gained during the course. 

Scope after BBA in Business Analytics from NMIMS

A student graduating in BBA Business Analytics from NMIMS can work as an Assistant Business Analyst working on development of business models for companies. He/she works with cross-functional teams, to analyse or generate accurate business forecasts. 

He/she can also work as Assistant Project Manager to assist a company in planning, management and implementation of business projects. He/she can also work in the sphere of data collection as a Business Intelligence Associate to assist the team in data collection and to identify data anomalies. 

Final Thoughts

Internet usage in India has doubled from 20% in 2018 to 41% in 2019. According to the World Bank, it is expected that India will add over 900 million users by 2025. This much increase in internet consumption has led to the explosive growth of the data industry in India. The data analytics industry is projected to create over 11 million jobs by 2026 with an increase in investments in AI. Being proficient in business analytics can help students to build a rewarding career in this fast-growing field. It offers a unique blend of technical and management skills that makes a young professional fit for roles across domains in a company.

Skills Learnt in a BBA Degree

How to access the assignments?

In under 3 steps, students can access the assignments on the student portal 

Step 1: – Log in to your NMIMS Student Portal 
Step 2: – Click on the Exam Tab 
Step 3: – A drop-down “Assignment” section will be available

Things to keep in mind while submitting the assignments 

The student portal created by NMIMS is user-friendly and has all there is to know about the assignments. The institute has uploaded videos for the students to understand each step.

 Things to keep in mind while submitting the assignments 

  1. In every cycle of the assignment submission process, there are new questions 
  2. The assignment should be written in MS Word and uploaded in PDF format.
  3. Hand-written or scanned copies are not accepted
  4. 0% plagiarism is expected from the students. They can take references but the content should not be copied. 
  5. The assignment consists of 3 questions. The First 2 questions of 10 marks and 3rd divided into 2 parts of 5 marks each. 
  6. The students are supposed to write only answers.
  7. All questions are mandatory 
  8. There are 2 free attempts for assignment submission
  9. The font style should be Arial or Times New Roman with a font size of 12

Steps to upload the assignment 

Step 1: – Tap on the “Assignment” section
Step 2: – Click on “Submit Assignment” Section
Step 3: – Attach your PDF document
Step 4: – And finally click on “Submit Assignment” 

Important FAQs 

1. How to convert a word file into a PDF

There are various online platforms where you can convert your word file into PDF for free, such as ilovepdf.com or smallpdf.com.

2. If I miss the deadline can I still submit the assignment? 

You can submit the assignment in the next cycle where the questions will differ. 

3. Can I take references from different resources?

Yes, you take references from any resource you want but the content should not be plagiarized. 

4. What are the repercussions of not submitting the assignment? 

If you have a zero marks case in your assignment due to plagiarism or non-submission, your entire result is put on hold.
