LATEST UPDATE ON CBSE 10TH RESULT:

Because of the increase of Covid cases, the 10th-grade exams were canceled. In addition, Covid-19 is delaying the review of CBSE class 10 results 2021. Officials lack the staff to create and upload results to the system in a timely manner. As a result, the CBSE 10th result for 2021 would be delayed. For the time being, the CBSE class 10 results 2021 are expected to be released in the final week of July.

The CBSE 10th exams were scheduled to take place in April, but they were postponed. Exams for the 12th grade have been postponed, and a new exam date has yet to be announced. After the examinations, the results of the 12th class will be released. However, the only thing that the students in the tenth grade are waiting for is the result

Where can I see the result of the CBSE 10TH Class Online?

Each year, the CBSE results are available on a variety of websites. However, we will only discuss the official CBSE portals in this article. We don’t know if third-party websites will have the CBSE results for this year or not. We’ve included the websites that will host CBSE 10th results in the section below.

www.cbse.nic.in\ www.cbseresults.nic.in \ www.results.nic.in

How do I check my CBSE 10th grade result for the year 2021?

  1. The initial step for students is to go to cbseresults.nic.in, which is the official CBSE results portal. There will be a list of results that have already been released.
  2. Now, go to the “CBSE CLASS X RESULT 2021” page and wait for it to load.
  3. Now Fill in the information requested on the page and click the Get Result button.
  4. Your final grade, broken down by subject, will be presented on the screen.
