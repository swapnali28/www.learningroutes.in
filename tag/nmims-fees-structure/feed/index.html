NMIMS-NGASCE Distance MBA Fee Structure

NMIMS is one of the best distance MBA institutes in terms of return on investment. The fee structure is quite affordable for the quality of education provided. Following is the fee structure for the Distance MBA program of 2 years and 4 semesters.

Students have the option of full fee payment, annual payment, or semester-wise payment and avail of a substantial discount on the first two options. The fee structure is also dependent on the mode of lecture delivery chosen by candidates that are prime and pro, subject-wise in each semester. With pro mode, students have access to live-interactive lectures as well as recorded lectures whereas in prime mode students have access to only recorded lectures but they can add live lectures for individual subjects at a fee of INR 1,200

  • An initial amount of 5000 INR from the program fees will be collected at the time of registration
  • The one-time Admission processing fee is 1,200 INR
  • Students need to pay an examination fee of 600 INR per subject
  • The University offers special incentives to the armed forces with a 20% concession.
  • Students also have the option to avail loan facility to pay program fees even without a credit card.
  • HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Citi Bank, Standard Charted Bank, HSBC Bank, SBI, and Kotak Mahindra Bank provides EMI facility (3,6,9,12 months) via credit cards.
  • The fee structure for distance learning programs are subject to change at the discretion of the University
  • Payment has to be made via online mode or Demand Draft made in favor of the SVKM’s NMIMS payable at Mumbai.

All in all, a candidate doesn’t have to worry about NMIMS fees. It is actually affordable and can be paid in no cost EMIs.
