]]> Do you work full-time and want to advance your career by enrolling in an MBA program but don’t have the time to attend regular classes? You could also be a recent graduate looking for work while completing your studies.

An online education MBA program may be the best option for you if you are unable to attend traditional college classes. By enrolling in an MBA program, you can pursue a career in business management.

The MBA is the only degree program that continues to gain popularity year after year. In a variety of ways, an MBA from a respectable university can help you enhance your career. The MBA and EMBA are the two most popular business management programs offered by Indian institutions and business schools.

If you’re not sure which university to enroll in for a distance MBA program, we can help. Continue reading this post if you want to learn about the top 10 finest distance education MBA programs in India.

IMT Ghaziabad

With a distance education MBA program, IMT Ghaziabad is one of India’s finest business schools. The institute, which was formed around a decade ago, has been recognized by the DEB University Grant Commission. In a short period of time, the institute has developed a great reputation.

IMT CDL offers a two-year Post Graduate Diploma in Management via online study (PGDM). The IMT CDL PGDM is regarded by many large businesses as being similar to an MBA program.

Indira Gandhi National Open University

IGNOU is India’s oldest and most illustrious open university, giving answers for more than five decades. The university has a large student database and is noted for its well-designed distance learning solutions. The IGNOU MBA is one of India’s most prestigious and well-received distance education programs in the discipline of business management.

It is mandatory to have completed grades 10, 10+2 and graduated from an accredited school or college. Candidates can apply online or in person at any of the city’s IGNOU locations.

NMIMS

NMIMS, or the most exceptional college in the online learning field, has received NAAC Grade ‘A’ accreditation. The university provides its students with freedom and convenience, as well as a magnificent distance learning education setup.

NMIMS, like other educational institutions, requires applicants to have completed grades 10, 10+2 and graduated from a recognized high school or college. In the selection process for NIT distant learning, work experience is given priority.

Amity University

When it comes to deliverables and quality education, Amity University is among the best of the best. Unlike other private institutions, Amity has raised its standards to the point that it has been able to distinguish itself from the rest of the pack.

Amity University’s online MBA program is a two-year degree program with 14 industry specializations. To be eligible for the Amity distance MBA program, an applicant must have a bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 40% marks.

Annamalai University

Annamalai University, located in South India, is one of the world’s oldest and most prestigious universities. The institution offers a variety of specialities to its students, most of which are based on industry standards.

Candidates must have a grade of ten, a grade of ten plus two, and a diploma from a recognized high school or college. Candidates can apply online through the university’s website or visit the college campus to get a feel for the environment and meet the teachers.

Sikkim Manipal University 

SMU is one of India’s first universities which provide distant learning programs. One of the most popular courses offered by SMU-DE is the distance education MBA program. The course format is based on unique industry standards and created by competent industry practitioners.

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program at SMU-DE is a two-year degree program with four MBA specializations.

MIT Pune

A unique project management tool inspired MIT’s distance learning education program, which simplifies the learning process for students on the other side of the world.

Candidates must hold a valid tenth, twelfth, and thirteenth-grade diploma from a recognized school or college. Candidates can apply either online through the company’s website or directly to the institution.

Prin. L. N. Welingkar Institute of Management

Another notable B-School in India is the Welingkar Institute of Management Development (WeSchool). The institute first opened its doors in 1977. Welingkar Institute of Management Development, in addition to its full-time regular courses, also offers part-time courses, such as postgraduate diploma in management, diploma, and certification courses, using a hybrid learning approach.

The University Grants Commission has accredited WeSchool’s distance learning courses (UGC-DEB). The MBA (PGDM) program at WeSchool is one of India’s most popular remote learning management programs. Welingkar University may be an excellent option for you if you’re seeking a good distance MBA in Mumbai.

ICFAI University

ICFAI University is a well-known name in the field of distance learning. ICFAI has been providing higher education in India for three decades and has received numerous honours for its educational excellence.

In the subject of business management, ICFAI provides a variety of degrees, postgraduate diplomas, and certificate programs. It is a well-known distance education MBA degree program with five modules. Electives make up the third module. Students must choose one elective from a list of sixteen options.

]]> Master of Business Administration (MBA) is one specialized professional course that trains students in the art of business organization and management. Over the years more and more professionals are pursuing MBA as the job opportunities for MBA graduates remain fairly high. But, pursuing a full-time MBA is not a feasible option for all as working professionals would have to leave their present job to do so which is why a distance MBA course is a better option. One of the major advantages of distance MBA is its affordable fee structure, industry-relevant curriculum, easy accessibility, digital education, flexible schedule, and effective time management. Moreover, during the Covid-19 pandemic when all classes are going on online even in full-time MBA courses then a distance MBA program is an equally good option for working professionals and MBA aspirants.

Institutions are eligible to offer distance learning programs at the undergraduate, masters, diploma or certification level as open and distance learning programs if it is duly approved by the statutory authority.  Such online Distance learning programs require a minimum National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) score of 3.26 on the 4-point scale. Many public and private colleges offer distance MBA programs but there are only a few that offer good value for your time and effort. So, while deciding on distance MBA candidates should properly research the institution and the program. Here is a list of few universities that offer the best distance learning MBA courses in India.

1. NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education

NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education, the distance and online learning platform of SVKM’s NMIMS Deemed-to-be University, is a leader in professional and continuing education. It is one of India’s largest autonomous top B schools with a NIRF Ranking of 24th in 2020.

  • NMIMS Distance MBA programs are approved and recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Distance Education Bureau (UGC-DEB) of India
  • The university has achieved the NAAC Accreditation ‘A+’ Grade with a CGPA of 3.59
  • It also provides first-its-kind career services in online education built for students and alumni of MBA and diploma programs to prepare them for a lifetime of career possibilities. The institute has tie-up with 300+ corporates to provide students an interview opportunity along with student support and career assistance.
  • Students have access to recorded lectures and the option to attend live-interactive classes via different modes of learning i.e. prime and pro
  • Networking is an important aspect of MBA and the program also provides access to the alumni portal of NMIMS
  • Students get a chance to learn from the experienced faculties of the NMIMS main campus and the degree received is directly from the NMIMS campus.

2. IMT-Centre for Distance Learning Ghaziabad

Institute of Management Technology (IMT) is one of the prestigious institutes in the country that offer distance MBA courses under its Center for Distance Learning (IMT CDL). It has over three decades of expertise in conducting management and education programs.

  • The distance learning programs of IMT CDL are approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE)
  • With more than 30,000 alumni across the globe, students of distance learning get a chance to expand their network with the alumni portal.
  • The post graduate diploma certification is provided from IMT-CDL
  • The program provides intensive online learning sessions, live interactive lectures, and a video library of recorded lectures of eminent faculty.
  • State of the art infrastructure with industry-ready curriculum and students can learn from highly experienced faculty.

3. Welingkar Institute of Management

Welingkar Institute of Management Development (WeSchool) based in Mumbai is another top school for distance MBA which is approved by AICTE. As per the NIRF ranking of 2020, the institute is among the top 50 B-schools of India.

  • It provides 18 hybrid PGDM programs which include 24 month PG and 6 month s certificate program in different field of management
  • The institute has launched different modes of imparting education including virtual classroom sessions, video lectures, personal contact programs, and online video libraries as well.
  • The distance MBA in hybrid format aims at experiential learning, collaborative learning, through interactive workshops, industrial visits, business simulation

4. Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU)

It is a well-reputed open university with a large number of student enrolments. Starting in 1987, the IGNOU school of management studies offers 9 programs in management and 13 programs in commerce.

  • The program follows a multimedia approach of delivery which includes self-learning printed course material, audio-video programs, and face to face interaction with academic counselors at the study centers
  • To be eligible for the course, students should be a graduate with at least 50% marks for the general category and 45% for the reserved category. Candidates should also clear the OPENMAT exam conducted by IGNOU, to be eligible for the course.
  • The program is designed in a modular format offering maximum flexibility and multiple exit points. It also provides multiple specialization options.
  • The minimum duration to complete the course is 2.5 years also the passing ratio is not very good.

5. Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning (SCDL)

Symbiosis is a well-known institute that provides a distance learning program through its Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning (SCDL) in various fields of management studies.

  • It is an autonomous stand-alone institute approved by AICTE that offers distance learning programs.
  • The mode of teaching includes contemporary delivery styles with a combination of printed study books, online classroom learning, e-learning, e-mentoring, and faculty interaction.
  • The faculties of the program are from SCDL course is not at par with the Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) 

6. ICFAI University

The Institute of Chartered Financial Analysts of India (ICFAI) Foundation for Higher Education, Hyderabad has been a pioneer for over 30 years in higher education programs. It provides an online MBA program through its Center for Distance and Online Education (CDOE)

  • ICFAI Group has a large team faculty pool coming from a diverse field of academic, industry, consulting, and research.
  • It is declared as a Deemed-to-be university and recognized by UGC
  • The university has achieved the NAAC Accreditation ‘A+’ Grade with a CGPA of 3.43
  • The mode of learning is through recorded videos, webinars, workbooks, and projects.
  • ICFAI provides students with the choice of any elective or combination of electives offered from any area for better program outcomes.
  • The entire distance MBA program is based on a self-study and examination model with a convenient and flexible schedule for exams

7. Amity University

Amity has been established by an act of State Legislature and recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC). It is one of the reputed institutions for a distance MBA program.

  • The distance learning program is offered in different specialization and regulated by the Amity Directorate of Distance and Online Education (ADDOE)
  • It has received accreditations from UGC, AICTE, and grade ‘A’ status from NAAC.
  • Students can avail themselves of live classes from international faculties and career opportunities at virtual job fairs.

8. Annamalai University

Based in Tamil Nadu, Annamalai University is one of the oldest and most reputed universities which offers several specialization courses to its students under its Directorate of Distance Education which are crafted to meet industry standards. The university has been accredited with grade ‘A’ status by NAA

9. Sikkim Manipal University- Distance Education (SMU-DE)

It is among the oldest universities to offer distance learning programs in India. The SMU-DE distance education Master of Business Administration (MBA) course is a two-year degree course that offers 4 MBA specializations which are marketing, finance, human resource management, and information systems. The minimum duration to complete the course is 2 years and the maximum is 4 years. It is accredited by NAAC.

  • The mode of learning is through self-learning material, 24/7 access to webinars, online classes, and counselors, ensuring that students get instant support whenever needed.
  • The necessary eligibility criteria are 10+2 and graduation in any field. Apart from this, students have to clear the entrance test conducted by the University for Admission. 

10. Lovely Professional University

Lovely Professional University is a UGC approved university located in Punjab and offers both regular and distance education courses. The distance MBA program is one of the top-ranked degree courses in the field of business management. LPU offers flexible learning and one on one faculty interaction as well. The minimum duration to complete the program is 2 years and the maximum is 4 years. Admissions to the course are done based on management entrance exams such as CAT, NMAT, GMAT, etc along with graduation percentage.

For working professionals and MBA aspirants who want to up-skill themselves and climb the corporate ladder, pursuing some of the best distance learning MBA course is a good option. The program will provide necessary acumen and holistic development while allowing one to continue their job and also not putting an extravagant financial burden.

Notably, the acceptability of distance learning courses post COVID19 is wider than ever. It has been observed across the globe that what matters in modern times is one’s ability to accomplish a task. In other words, one’s skills are put to test rather than having a look at their degree or certificate. In such a case, one must carefully see the curriculum for distance learning courses, which must be industry-aligned and crafted to solely aim to impart in-demand market skills to the candidate pursuing it.
