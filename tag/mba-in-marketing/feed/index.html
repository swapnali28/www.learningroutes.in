The MBA is a two-year program. You can select one of your favorite and most interesting specializations from finance, marketing, human resources, operations management, and so on. One of the most popular Masters of Business Administration specializations is marketing (MBA). Every year, a big number of students around the country choose to pursue an MBA in Marketing.

Here, we’re discussing one of the specialization i.e. “Marketing.”

The following courses may be included in a marketing MBA:

  • Strategic Planning
  • Marketing Campaigns
  • Market Research
  • Cost Volume and Profit
  • Consumer Behavior
  • Analysis of Demand

During your study, you will also learn communication skills, analytical skills, the capacity to combine programs or concepts, corporate ethics, and leadership.

To get admitted to an MBA program with any specialization, you must take the GMAT 2021/CMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test/Combined Management Admission Test) and pass it.

After completing a bachelor’s degree in engineering, medicine, science, art, or humanities, you can pursue an MBA in finance.

Scope Of MBA in Marketing

MBA in Marketing has a lot of potentials. Marketing is a broad field in and of itself. After completing an MBA in marketing, a candidate can work as a sales professional or manager for reputable FMCG businesses such as HUL or Marico Industries. Working in the IT industry is also an option.

Retail, tourism, banking, hospitality, media, advertising, consulting, market research, and other industries all have job openings.

Candidates with an MBA in Marketing can also work as a lecturer in a reputed university. There are a number of top-rated B-schools in India that pay their professors well. If you want to pursue higher education, you can enroll in a Ph.D. program in a relevant field.
