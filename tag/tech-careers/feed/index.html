1. AI and Machine Learning Engineer 

An ML Engineer typically work in the data science team alongside Data Scientists, Administrators and Data Analysts. He is an individual who is engaged in the IT domain and focuses on researching and designing Artificial Intelligence(AI) systems to build predictive models.

Skills Required

  • Software Engineering Skills
  • Advanced Mathematics and Statistical Skills
  • Relevant experience in working with ML frameworks
  • Knowledge of Programming languages like Python, Java, C++ and Javascript

How to become an AI and Machine Learning Engineer? 

To begin your career in the AI and ML field, you need to have a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or any other relevant field along with an advanced degree in Data Science & Machine Learning along with all the above-mentioned skills.

2. UI/UX Designer

Today, every business has its own website or application in order to showcase themselves. UI/UX Designer is a person who helps businesses in creating interactive apps, websites or other social media platforms. Their job role includes collaborating with the different team members in designing the user interface ideas.

Skills Required

  • Professional Certification in User Experience(UX) and User Interface(UI), 
  • Communication & Presentation Skills
  • Expertise in software like Figora, Sketch, Adobe XD etc.

How to Become a UX/UI Designer?

If you have the zest to make your career in the designing field and have a creative mindset, you can surely make your career in the UI/UX field by upskilling yourself with the skills that are required for it. You can then create your work portfolios and explore great opportunities in the job market. For creative people, this is definitely one of the best jobs in future.

3. Robotics Engineer 

A Robotics Engineer is a person who designs, constructs and develops robots and robotics systems. They also maintain the software systems and incorporate the knowledge of diverse fields such as computer science, electronics and cognitive psychology to create efficient robotics systems.

Skills Required: 

  • Knowledge of Programming Languages 
  • Creative and Critical  Thinking Abilities 
  • Problem-Solving Abilities 
  • Mechanical Skills

How to Become a Robotics Engineer?

To become a Robotics Engineer, you should have a background in Computer Science/ Mechanical/ Electrical Engineering. As this is a highly technical field, you need an excellent knowledge of Mathematics, Calculus, Engineering, Physics and Algebra. You will also need a creative and adaptive mindset.

4. Data Scientist

The field of data science has gained tremendous popularity in recent times. But do you know the reason behind it? It is because major companies have large amounts of data stored in huge databases that need to be assessed to gain valuable insights. Thus, there is a rising demand for data scientists. Data Scientists are professionals who work on data analysis in order to understand business problems and find relevant solutions so as to increase the efficiency of operations. 

Skills Required: 

  • Knowledge of Computer Science, Data Science and Mathematics
  • Technical skills such as Machine Learning, Programming, Processing Large Data, and Statistical Analysis 
  • Experience in using technical tools and systems
  • Intermediate knowledge of Python, SQL

How to Become a Data Scientist?

The Data Science field is among the best career options for the future. Today, there are a plethora of options that can help you upskill and make a career in data science or switch to this new field. You can enrol yourself in a Data Science programme. It is offered by many top universities of India.

5. Cloud Engineer 

Do you know that about two-thirds of the big companies have made it a priority to transition their business apps and data storage to cloud services? Therefore, it is natural that the demand for cloud computing professionals is on a hike.

Cloud Engineers assess an organisation’s technology infrastructure and evaluate if it needs to move to the cloud system. They are also responsible for negotiating with the vendors and to coordinate with IT and senior members to supervise the cloud migration project.

Skills Required: 

  • Knowledge of Programming languages
  • Database and Network Management
  • Strong understanding of the Linux Operating System, DevOps and Cloud Security

How to Become a Cloud Engineer?

If you have experience in programming languages and possess technical skills with any certification or relevant course in the cloud system, you can easily step in to become a Cloud Engineer.

6. Cybersecurity Specialist

A Cybersecurity Specialist is a professional who is responsible for providing security to software and networks. He is also responsible for searching for any upcoming risks in systems and monitoring any attacks or intrusions.

Skills Required

  • Programming knowledge(C++, PHP, Java, Shell)
  • Certification in Cloud & Blockchain Security, Internet of things(IoT), Artificial Intelligence, Coding
  • Knowledge of Virtual Machines and Operating Systems

How to Become a Cybersecurity Specialist? 

If you have graduated or have a certification in Computer science, IT, Systems Engineering or any other similar field and have work experience in security operations, then you can opt for this promising job in future.
