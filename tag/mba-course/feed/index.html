MBA in Finance VS MBA in Finance & Banking – Which is better?

Both the MBA in Banking & Finance and the MBA in Finance prepare students for professions in the financial sector, including financial planning, investment, and corporate finance.

The main distinction between the two programs is that the MBA in Finance is focused on the core financial services sector and aims to fill significant gaps in the market. In order to prepare students for a successful career in the core finance, fintech, and financial services industries, the curriculum is designed on the basis of financial theory and its application.

The MBA in Banking and Financial Services is created to meet the expanding needs of management professionals working in the sector. The program’s goal is to create experts in quality management who are technically and managerially competent. The program’s main goal is to create a comprehensive and integrative management style for the banking and financial services sector.

Now let’s come down to the skills you will be able to acquire after your studies 

Financial Reporting & Analysis –

Understanding businesses is a big aspect of finance, and having a solid knowledge of accounting is necessary to achieve that properly. Even while you might not need to know how to create financial statements from scratch, being able to read them and make deductions from them is a valuable ability to have which you will get by doing the course.

Industry/Business Analysis –  

A course in banking will help you learn the industry and business as you will be going through equity reports which will be mainly Initiating Coverage. An IC report will enable you to have a comprehensive analysis of the business and the industries in the market. 

Financial Modeling –

A financial model is a tool that is a part of excel and is used to have a detailed analysis and valuation of the finances. Once a candidate will achieve a degree in Finance or Banking, he can easily build a 3-statement financial model and Discounted cash flow which is an important skill for finance professionals.
