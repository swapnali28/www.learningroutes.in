NMIMS Distance Education Highlights

NMIMS Distance Education MBA Admission 2023

The MBA program at NMIMS has been developed in such a way that the student can have fun while studying the material and meets industry standards. MBA is a master’s degree program at the professional level that covers all of the topics that are relevant in everyday life. Students must graduate from an accredited university with a bachelor’s degree in any subject. A minimum of 50% in prior academics is required of candidates.

Admission Procedure:

Step 1: Registration

Fill out the online Registration Form and pay the Admission Processing Fee.

Step 2: Document Submission

You’re required to submit the following documents to the Authorised Enrolment Partner.

  • Academic Documents
  • Photographic Identification (Govt. approved)
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Documents for Work Experience (if any)

The papers will be verified by the counselor at the Authorised Enrolment Partner and then uploaded to the system.

*At the time of admission, enrolling students must bring authentic documentation.

Step 3: Payment of Fees

Payment Options: 

  • Bank Demand Draft payable to ‘SVKM’s NMIMS’ in Mumbai.
  • Online payment using credit cards.

Step 4: Confirmation

When the University approves the supplied papers and fees, your admission will be verified, and you will be given a student number.

(On confirmation of acceptance, Study Kits, Student Portal Activation, and Lectures are available.)
