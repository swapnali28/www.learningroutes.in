The Deemed-to-be-University is known for their management courses with the top-notch e-learning infrastructure and well-qualified and professional faculty.

Why to choose NMIMS Distance Learning

Recognition:

NMIMS Distance Learning courses are well recognized by national and international organisations., qualifying the most important feature of any institute. The university has got all the important approvals and a top-notch level education council providing best programs to working professionals.

NMIMS Distance MBA is approved by UGC-DEB enabling it to deliver distance and online education. Also, it is approved by NAAC by grade A+. The university has scored 3.59 CGPA out of 4 on the NAAC scale.

Awards:

Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies received “Deemed to be University” status in the year 2003 by the University Grants Commission of India (UGC). NMIMS is Awarded Category 1 Autonomy by the UGC. NMIMS Distance MBA received various awards proving its excellence in the field of online education :-NMIMS has received the following recognitions and awards as well: –

· Top Distance Learning Institute in India for excellence in Management Education – Competition Success Review.

·Ranked 2nd among Top 10 B-Schools offering Distance Education – Zee Business.

·Ranked 5th in Top Distance Learning Institute in India – DNA Indus Learning Survey

Experiential Learning:

Students benefit from large, organized, and versatile case studies because NMIMS provides the best hands-on learning experience and develops their problem-solving ability. This type of learning helps the students gain the knowledge and aptitude to manage situations at a higher authority level.

Exam Modules:

 Exams based on MCQ, tasks, and long answer style questions offer a complex, application-oriented, versatile test schedule that allows working professionals with a tight work schedule to complete the curriculum. The flexibility to pick and select topics, dates, time-slots, and the possibility of appearing on more than one subject on the same day (based on slot availability).

 Alumni Connect:

Students often do not get the most out of an online class because it is difficult for them to engage and communicate with their peers and professors. NMIMS Distance Programs provide you the advantages of a traditional MBA by fostering and strengthening peer and tutor connections through a variety of activities and they also provide an Alumni Portal for the students at the end of 3rd semester for them to expand their network and look for job opportunities. . Students will be able to encounter a variety of business professionals as a result of this.

What makes NMIMS Distance Learning the Best program?

NMIMS distance learning program is favored among students and working professionals due to various reasons such as – the fee structure is pocket friendly, NMIMS provides “Career Assistance Services” along with “Alumni Portal” for students to connect with business professionals. They also offer various specialisations and their student portal is open 24*7 for the students to access the recordings anytime, anywhere.  

Why NMIMS Distance programs are suitable for working professionals and why they will reap better benefits from the program?

NMIMS Distance programs are specially designed for working professionals who want to enhance their skills and gather specialized knowledge to succeed in the future. Not only that, NMIMS Distance MBA provides better placement opportunities to working professionals as the hiring companies get working professionals who are already aware of the working environment, adding value to the company in many ways.
