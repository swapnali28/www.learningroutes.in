]]> As the 10+2 session comes to an end, students rush to get admission into their dream college. Everyone gets caught in the hustle-bustle of the whole procedure- from shortlisting colleges to college counseling.

Among science students, many desire to pursue engineering in a prestigious college. A lot of planning and contemplation goes behind choosing the perfect engineering college for yourself, many aspects are to be kept in mind while selecting-

  • All india rankings
  • Curriculum structure
  • Faculty
  • Placements
  • Teaching methodologies
  • Campus
  • Co-scholastic activities
  • Research and internship exposure
  • Collaborations with other institutes

In this article, we will be covering the top 10 engineering colleges of India to make the process of college selection slightly easy for you.

IIT BOMBAY

Number 1 on the list is, no other than the prestigious, IIT Bombay. It was established in 1958 with the help of UNESCO and the former Soviet Union. IIT Bombay B.Tech is a flagship program of the institute. It is a 4-year program with a total course fee of INR 9.18 lacs. Admission to this course is based on the rank obtained in the JEE Advanced Exam. The institute is also known for a plethora of startup opportunities and excellent placements. 

IIT MADRAS

Since the past 5 years, IIT Madras has been ranked no.1 in NIRF rankings. Out of 121 courses, the B.Tech program offered by the institute is highly popular. Admission to this course is done through the JEE Advance exam. The placement cell of the institute has a strong network with the industries, ensuring a holistic exposure to students. The annual fee for the B.Tech program is INR 2.05 lacs.

IIT DELHI

IIT Delhi is ranked second in NIRF rankings among the top engineering colleges. The institute is well-known for its collaborations and MOUs. It has 33 national and 101 international MOUs in academia and research. The semester fee for the Btech program is INR 1.24 lacs. To get admission into this program, students need to get a certain rank in the JEE advance exam. The placements of IIT Delhi are excellent with a placement rate of 70%.

IIT KHARAGPUR

IIT Kharagpur secures 5th place in the NIRF rankings among the top engineering colleges. It has the largest campus among all the IITs in India. The B.tech program is offered under 15 specializations. The semester fee for this program is 1.34 lacs. IIT KGP offers students the option to switch to a dual degree program at the end of the 3rd year.

INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY, HYDERABAD

Established in 1998, IIT Hyderabad is an autonomous university. It is a not-for-profit public-private partnership institute. Their B.tech in Artificial Intelligence and B.tech in CSE programs are quite popular. The admission is done through various exams such as JEE Mains, KVPY, etc. The placements of the institute are excellent with 100% placement rate of its recent batch. The average package offered is INR 25 LPA. Top recruiters at IIIT Hyderabad are- Amazon, Adobe, Google, and so on.

BIRLA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND SCIENCE, PILANI

BITS Pilani is a renowned Institute which holds a special place in the list of top engineering colleges of India. BITS Pilani has been ranked 26th among engineering colleges in the NIRF rankings. Admission to this institute is based on the score obtained in the entrance test BITSAT conducted by the institute. The fee for B.E is around INR 19 lacs. top recruiters include- Facebook, Cisco Systems, Accenture, and so on. BITS Pilani has recorded the highest salary package of INR 1.33 CPA.

IIT, GUWAHATI

IIT Guwahati is ranked 7th among engineering colleges in the NIRF rankings.  Its campus is considered to be the most beautiful campus of the country, with the Brahmaputra river and hills surrounding it. IIT Guwahati has 135 international MOUs. They offer students massive exposure with their collaborations, research projects, internships, and exchange programs. The admission to the B.Tech program is done on the basis of rank secured in JEE Advance. The total course fee is INR 8.54 lacs. The placements are excellent with Google, Microsoft, Adobe, etc., as their top recruiters

INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, BANARAS HINDU UNIVERSITY, VARANASI

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) is a central university located in the city of Varanasi. It is a NAAC ‘A’ grade university with recognition from UGC. BHU is ranked 3rd among top Indian universities by NIRF rankings. Admission is based on the score obtained in the entrance test conducted by the university. BHU has more than 40 national and international MOUs, providing students with a plethora of opportunities. The course fee for the B.tech program is INR 3.02 lacs. BHU also provides impressive placement opportunities. The highest package recorded was of INR 1.5 CPA.

NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, KARNATAKA

NIT Karnataka is ranked among the top 10 engineering institutes by NIRF rankings. B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a flagship program of NITK. The admission is done on the basis of rank obtained in JEE Main examination which is followed by JoSAA counseling. The first-year fee for this program is INR 1.53 lacs. NITK has a placement record of 80% each year. Flipkart, Apple, Intel, etc. are the top recruiters of the institute.

NETAJI SUBHASH UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, DELHI

NSUT Delhi, previously known as Netaji Subhash Institute of Technology (NSIT) Delhi, is a prestigious university located in the capital city. This institute is a front runner for the B.tech in Computer and Science Engineering program. The admission to this program is done on the basis of scores obtained in JEE Main exam followed by JAC Delhi counseling. The first-year fee for the course is INR 1.66 lacs. Top recruiters include- Amazon, Reliance, Jio, KPMG, etc- ensuring excellent placements for students.
