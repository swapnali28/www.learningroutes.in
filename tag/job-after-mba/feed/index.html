]]> The Distance MBA program is becoming a popular choice among prospective students in India. It is a viable alternative for working professionals and MBA aspirants who do not want to quit their jobs as the program provides greater flexibility for the student to pursue an MBA degree and a full-time job simultaneously. A distance learning program will save the opportunity cost that you would bear if you were unemployed for the two years spent on a full-time MBA program. It also saves valuable resources like time, money, and energy invested in a regular MBA course. The course curriculum, business, and managerial skills acquired in the program are at par with a regular MBA which is why more and more students are applying for distance MBA every year.

But, a major concern that students have when they contemplate a distance MBA program is the value of the degree when it comes to employment opportunities. Top B-schools like NMIMS offer specialized distance MBA programs along with first-its-kind career services in online education built for students and alumni of PG and diploma programs to prepare them for a lifetime of career possibilities. The institute has tie-up with leading corporates to provide students an interview opportunity along with student support and career assistance.

Career Options after MBA

A distance MBA program aims to upskill students and with the up-gradation of skills comes better opportunities and career growth. The placement scenario is not the same as in a full-time MBA course but students get to expand their network and skillset which would help them to land a better job.

After completing a Distance MBA course from a renowned university, candidates can explore various opportunities in the corporate world. Some of the roles offered by the companies are executive roles in Finance, Marketing, Corporate Associate, Portfolio Manager, Finance Analyst, Credit Risk Manager, HR manager, Employee Relations Manager, Brand Manager, Sales Manager, Digital Media Manager, and many other roles in different departments of the organization.

Distance MBA graduates can get suitable opportunities not only in the private sector but also in government sectors. They are eligible to apply to different government sectors like Banks, Railways, Airports, and Public Sector Undertaking (PSUs), which keep updating job vacancies for the deserving MBA graduates. 

Based on the MBA specialization, here are some of the job roles and career opportunities for distance MBA graduates in India.

Distance MBA-Marketing:

Marketing is one of the most popular specialization choices for distance MBA. After graduation, some of the roles offered by recruiters are Marketing Executive, Product Manager, Market Researcher, Brand Manager, Asset Manager, Corporate Sales, Media Planner, Online Marketing, Retailing Management, Customer Relations Manager, Corporate Communications Head, Digital Media Analyst, Analytical Marketing, Competitive Marketing, Media Manager, Marketing Manager, Channel Head, and Public Relations Manager.

Distance MBA-Finance: 

The finance department is most important in all organizations. The career options available are Cash Manager, Manager Consultants, Investment Banking Associates, Credit Managers and Specialist, Financial Analyst, Corporate Controllers, Finance Officers and Treasures, Insurance and Risk Manager, Financial Auditor, Financial Planner, Budget Analyst, Bank Manager, and Investment Banker. The top recruiters in this field are EY, HDFC Bank, Accenture, TCS, and ICICI Bank

Distance MBA-Human Resource Management: 

HR is responsible for recruiting, training, orienting, and apprising the employees of the company. The job profiles include HR Manager, HR Generalist, Staffing Director, Technical Recruiter, Compensation Manager, Employee Relations Manager, Employment Manager, HR Consultant, HR Specialist, HR Executive, Employee Education Consultant, and International HR Professional.

Distance MBA-Information Technology and Systems Management:

 IT is another booming sector offering promising careers to distance MBA graduates as a Project Manager, Business Development Manager, Marketing Manager, Corporate Strategy Manager, Analytics Manager, System Manager, Data Processing Manager, Business Analyst, and Technical IT Consultants. Some of the top recruiters in the field are Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Infosys, HCL Technologies, Wipro, Cognizant, Accenture, etc.

Distance MBA-International Business:

For students with a Distance MBA degree in International Business, the chances of getting an international jobs increases. The job profiles in the field are Administrative Service Manager, International Trade Manager, Business Development Manager, Policy Analyst, Freight Auditor, Business Analyst, Export and Import Management, Export Coordinator, and Foreign Exchange Management.  Some of the recruiters in the field are Amazon, Walmart, Cognizant, Goldman Sachs, Deloitte, Wipro, Accenture, TCS, BCG, etc.

Entrepreneurship/Start-up: The scope of entrepreneurship is tremendous in a developing country like India and many graduates venture into start-ups and as it holds vast opportunities.
