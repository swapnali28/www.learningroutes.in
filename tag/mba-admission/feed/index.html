In order to make the enrolment process easier, NMIMS is spread across 30 cities in India with 9 regional offices and over 80 Authorised Enrolment Partners. In this article we discuss, all admission-related queries of the NMIMS Distance MBA program.

Eligibility Criteria for NMIMS distance MBA:

For admission to the NMIMS distance MBA program, the eligibility criteria are a bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% marks in graduation or even less than 50% marks are also acceptable with a minimum of 2 years of work experience.

NMIMS Admission Process

The NMIMS distance MBA is a two-year program divided into four semesters. The admission process for the program is quite simple. It is only a four-step process.

Step1: Complete the online registration form and pay the mentioned admission processing fee of 1,200 INR

Step2: Make a visit to the nearest Authorised Enrolment Partner along with the below-mentioned original documents. The counselor at the center will verify the documents and upload them to the system.

  • Academic Documents
  • Work experience Document (if any)
  • Photo Identity
  • Passport Size photograph 

Step3: Make the course fee payment via an online transaction or Demand Draft in favor of ‘SVKM’s NMIMS’ payable at Mumbai.

  • Students also have the option to avail loan facility to pay program fees even without a credit card.
  • HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Citi Bank, Standard Charted Bank, HSBC Bank, SBI, and Kotak Mahindra Bank provides EMI facility (3,6,9,12 months) via credit cards.

Step4: A ‘student number’ will be issued when the submitted documents and the payment are approved which marks confirmation of admission in the program. Upon confirmation study kit is dispatched, student portal and lectures are activated.

Students also have the option of lateral entry to distance MBA from other diploma or PGP programs and they can apply during the same period as new admissions. Following are some of the important instructions for lateral entry students:

  • Eligibility criteria for the program must be checked before applying for the program
  • The students who are yet to appear or are awaiting diploma/PGP results are not eligible to apply.
  • Course waiver policy will be applicable for students only within 2 years of successful completion of their earlier program
  • Students have to complete any of the new courses added which are not available in the old structure for lateral admission
  • Course waiver policy is not applicable for theproject
  • There is no fee reduction on lateral admission
  • Candidates applying for lateral admission will have to repay the registration fee and will be treated as fresh admissions.
