]]> MBA entrance tests are used to narrow down candidates for MBA admittance. In India, there are more than 50 entrance tests for major MBA programs. The most well-known is the CAT exam, which is administered by Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs). MBA admission exams in India are held at three different levels.

List of Exams

Candidates must understand the full scope of the MBA admission exams for which they are applying and prepare accordingly. While the CAT and XAT are the most famous MBA entrance examinations, there are a few more MBA entrance tests via which applicants can gain admission to prestigious Business Schools.

University Level popular entrance exams: XAT, SNAP, IIFT, IRMASAT.

State Level  popular MBA entrance exams: MAH-CET, TSICET, KMAT, TANCET, ATMA

National Level popular exams are: CAT, CMAT, MAT
